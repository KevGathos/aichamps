2. What Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Email address — used for email OTP sign-in (6-digit code sent to your email)
  • Google account — if you sign in via Google Sign-In
  • Display name — the name you choose to show on leaderboards
  • Age — to ensure age-appropriate access (minimum age: 10)
  • Phone number — optional, collected for scholarship M-Pesa payouts only. No SMS is sent.
  • Parent or guardian phone number — required for all users under 18 years of age
  • Country of residence — used to display amounts in your local currency
  • Referral code — if you were referred by another user or wish to refer others

2.2 Learning Data

  • Level completion progress (which levels you have completed)
  • Quiz scores and completion times
  • Championship entry records and rankings
  • Platform unlock status
  • Achievement badges and XP points

2.3 Payment Data

  • Google Play transaction reference numbers only
  • Platform unlock records and community sponsorship donation records
  • M-Pesa payout number — collected only from scholarship winners at the time of payout

We do not collect or store credit card numbers, bank account details, M-Pesa PINs, or Google Pay credentials. All payments are processed securely through Google Play Billing.

2.4 Device Data

  • FCM token — for push notifications (e.g. championship reminders)
  • Device model, OS version, app version — used for technical support only

We do not collect location data, browsing history, contacts, photos, or any data beyond what is listed above.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide access to the AI Champs learning curriculum and track your learning progress
  • To administer monthly AI Scholarship Championships and distribute scholarship awards via M-Pesa to winners
  • To authenticate your account securely via email OTP or Google Sign-In
  • To send important notifications including OTP codes, payment confirmations, and championship results
  • To display your display name and score publicly on the in-app leaderboard
  • To display amounts in your local currency based on your country of residence
  • To track referral activity and credit referral earnings to your account
  • To improve the app based on anonymised usage patterns
  • To enforce age-appropriate access and parental consent requirements
  • To comply with our legal obligations under the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Children and Parental Consent

AI Champs is designed for learners aged 10 and above. Users under the age of 10 are blocked from creating an account.

For all users under 18 years of age, a parent or guardian must provide consent before the learner can access any learning material.

  • A parent or guardian phone number is collected and recorded during account setup for users under 18
  • We collect only the minimum data necessary for the service
  • We do not serve behavioural or targeted advertising to users under 18
  • We do not share personal data of users under 18 with third parties for marketing purposes
  • If you believe a child under 10 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at geoinfortrak@gmail.com and we will delete the account promptly
  • Parents and guardians may request deletion of their child's account at any time by contacting us

6. Referral Programme

AI Champs includes an optional referral programme that allows users to earn credit toward a free platform unlock by referring new users.

  • Every user is assigned a unique 6-character referral code at signup
  • When a new user signs up using your referral code and unlocks a platform, you earn KES 10 referral credit
  • Referral credit accumulates in your account and can be redeemed for a free platform unlock once the threshold is reached
  • Referral activity is recorded in our database — who referred whom, and which platform was unlocked
  • Referral data is private — referred users do not see who referred them, and referrers do not see the full profile of those they referred
  • Referral credit has no cash value and cannot be withdrawn

7. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored securely on Supabase servers with HTTPS/TLS encryption on all data transfers
  • We use Row Level Security (RLS) so that each user can only access their own data
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Payment transactions are processed entirely through Google Play's secure billing platform — we never handle raw payment credentials
  • Scholarship payout M-Pesa numbers are collected only at the time of award and used only for that single transaction
  • In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities as required by the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019

8. Your Rights

Under the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access: Your learning progress, scores, and profile information are visible within the app. You may also request a full copy by emailing geoinfortrak@gmail.com
  • Right to correction: You can update your display name and other profile details from within the app's profile screen. For other corrections, contact us by email
  • Right to deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated personal data (see Section 9)
  • Right to object: Object to the processing of your data for specific purposes
  • Right to portability: Request your data in a portable format by emailing geoinfortrak@gmail.com

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at geoinfortrak@gmail.com. We will respond within 21 days.

9. Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time from within the app. All personal data will be anonymised immediately upon deletion. Payment records are retained for 90 days as required by Kenyan financial regulations, then permanently deleted.

9.1 In-App Deletion

Open AI Champs → Profile tab → scroll to "Danger Zone" → tap "Delete My Account". Upon confirmation:

  • Your display name, phone number, age, and personal details are anonymised immediately
  • Your account is marked as deleted and you are signed out
  • Referral earnings and referral history are permanently deleted
  • Payment records are retained for 90 days as required by Kenyan financial regulations, then permanently deleted
  • You may contact geoinfortrak@gmail.com within 30 days to recover your account

9.2 Email Deletion Request

If you cannot access the app, send an email to geoinfortrak@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My Account" and include the email or phone number associated with your account. We will process your request within 7 business days.

10. Scholarship Awards and Financial Data

  • Championship entry is free. No payment is required to compete

  • The M-Pesa payout number does not need to match the login phone number

  • Refund requests for in-app purchases should be submitted directly through Google Play
