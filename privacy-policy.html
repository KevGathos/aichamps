2. What Information We Collect

  • Account information: Display name, phone number or email address used as login, and age
  • Parent or guardian information: Phone number of parent or guardian, required for all users under 18 years of age
  • Payment information: Google Play transaction reference numbers only. We do NOT store card numbers, M-Pesa PINs, or full payment credentials
  • Learning progress: Level completion data, quiz scores, achievement badges, and XP points
  • Championship data: Competition entries, leaderboard rankings, and scholarship award history
  • Payout information: M-Pesa number provided by winners at time of award — collected only when a user wins a scholarship
  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, and app version — used for technical support only

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide access to the AI Champs learning curriculum and track your learning progress
  • To administer monthly AI Scholarship Championships and distribute scholarship awards via M-Pesa to winners
  • To send important account notifications including password resets, payment confirmations, and championship results
  • To improve the app based on anonymised usage patterns
  • To enforce age-appropriate access and parental consent requirements
  • To comply with our legal obligations under the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019

4. Children and Parental Consent

AI Champs is designed for learners aged 10 and above. Users under the age of 10 are blocked from creating an account.

For all users under 18 years of age, a parent or guardian must provide consent before the learner can access any learning material.

  • A parent or guardian phone number is collected and recorded during account setup for users under 18
  • We collect only the minimum data necessary for the service
  • We do not serve behavioural or targeted advertising to users under 18
  • We do not share personal data of users under 18 with third parties for marketing purposes
  • Parents and guardians may request deletion of their child's account at any time by contacting geoinfortrak@gmail.com

5. How We Share Your Information

We do NOT sell your personal information to anyone, ever.

We share data only with the following service providers, strictly for the purpose of operating AI Champs:

Service Provider Purpose
Supabase Database and authentication — stores account and learning progress data securely
Google Play App distribution and in-app payment processing
Safaricom M-Pesa / Daraja API Processing scholarship award payments to winners
Twilio Sending SMS verification codes during phone number signup

We may also disclose data to legal authorities in Kenya when required by law. Our data safety practices are also disclosed in the Google Play Data Safety section of our app listing.

6. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored securely on Supabase servers with HTTPS encryption on all data transfers
  • We use Row Level Security (RLS) so that each user can only access their own data
  • Payment transactions are processed entirely through Google Play's secure billing platform — we never handle raw payment credentials
  • Scholarship payout M-Pesa numbers are collected only at the time of award and used only for that single transaction
  • We retain your data for as long as your account is active, or as required by Kenyan law
  • In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities as required by the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019

7. Your Rights

Under the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Right to correction: Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Right to deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated personal data
  • Right to object: Object to the processing of your data for specific purposes
  • Right to portability: Request your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at geoinfortrak@gmail.com. We will respond within 21 days.

8. Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time. All your personal data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days of your request.

To delete your account, use any of these methods:

  • In the app: Go to Profile tab → Settings → Delete Account
  • By email: Send a deletion request to geoinfortrak@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request"
  • Online: kevgathos.github.io/aichamps/privacy-policy.html

Upon deletion, all personal data including display name, phone number, email address, learning progress, championship history, and payout information will be permanently deleted.

9. In-App Purchases and Payments

  • All in-app purchases are processed securely through Google Play Billing
  • Platform unlocks (KES 350 each) are one-time purchases — no subscriptions, no recurring charges, no expiry
  • Scholarship championship entry is always free — no payment is required to compete
  • Scholarship awards are paid via Safaricom M-Pesa to verified winners only
  • Refund requests for in-app purchases should be submitted directly through Google Play

10. Cookies and Advertising

  • AI Champs does not use cookies
  • The app does not track your activity across other apps or websites
  • The app does not display advertisements of any kind
  • We do not use your data for targeted advertising

11. Changes to This Policy

Continued use of AI Champs after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
