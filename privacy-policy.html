2. What Information We Collect

  • Account information: Display name, phone number or email (used as login), and age range
  • Parent/Guardian information: Phone number of parent or guardian (required for users under 18)
  • Payment information: Google Play transaction reference numbers. We do NOT store card numbers, M-Pesa PINs, or full payment credentials
  • Learning progress: Level completion data, quiz scores, achievement badges, and XP points
  • Championship data: Competition entries, rankings, and scholarship award history
  • Payout information: M-Pesa number provided by winners at time of award — collected only when a user wins
  • Device information: Device model, OS version, and app version (for technical support only)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide access to the AI Champs learning curriculum and track your progress
  • To administer monthly scholarship championships and distribute awards via M-Pesa to winners
  • To send important account notifications (password resets, payment confirmations, championship results)
  • To improve the app based on anonymised usage patterns
  • To comply with our legal obligations under the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019

4. Children's Privacy (Under 13)

AI Champs is designed for learners aged 10 and above. For users under 13 years of age:

  • A parent or guardian must provide consent before account creation
  • We collect only the minimum data necessary
  • We do not serve behavioural advertising to users under 13
  • We do not share children's personal data with third parties for marketing purposes
  • Parents may request deletion of their child's account by contacting geoinfortrak@gmail.com

5. How We Share Your Information

We do NOT sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Supabase — database and authentication provider for storing account and progress data
  • Google Play — for app distribution and in-app payment processing
  • Safaricom M-Pesa / Daraja API — for processing scholarship award payments to winners
  • Twilio — for sending SMS verification codes during phone signup
  • Legal authorities — only when required by law in Kenya

6. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers with HTTPS encryption on all transfers
  • We use Row Level Security (RLS) so users can only access their own data
  • Payment transactions are processed through Google Play's secure billing platform
  • We retain your data for as long as your account is active, or as required by Kenyan law

7. Your Rights (Kenya Data Protection Act 2019)

  • Right to access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Right to correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Right to deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Right to object: Object to processing of your data for specific purposes

To exercise these rights: geoinfortrak@gmail.com

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via the app. Continued use of AI Champs after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
